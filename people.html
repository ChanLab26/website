The Team

The Neural Circuits & Neurotechnology Laboratory brings together clinician-scientists, engineers, and trainees united by one goal: understanding and healing the human brain.

Principal Investigator

Ho Wing (Andy) Chan, MD

Assistant Professor of Neurology & Neuroscience
Rockefeller Neuroscience Institute · Department of Neurology · West Virginia University

Dr. Chan is a physician-scientist epileptologist with a passion for brain-computer interfaces (BCI). He trained under Dr. Helen Mayberg at Mount Sinai, a pioneer of deep brain stimulation for depression, where he developed his foundational interest in circuit-based neuromodulation. His lab's work spans three areas, in order of emphasis: brain-computer interfaces for communication and rehabilitation; neuromodulation for epilepsy and mood; and other neurotechnologies, including in-ear EEG and digital biomarkers.
